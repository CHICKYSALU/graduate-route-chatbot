About us

You should not have to figure it all out alone

The Graduate Route started with Fatima, our founder, sitting exactly where many students are now: excited about what could come next, but overwhelmed by how to get there. There were forms, deadlines, essays, scholarships, and plenty of advice, but very little that answered the questions she actually had. That is where The Graduate Route began: with a promise to offer students the honest guidance, encouragement, and personal investment Fatima had once been searching for herself.

Personal support from someone who knows your story

The person helping you shape your strategy is also the person reading your essays, reviewing your documents, and following your progress. Your application is not passed between different people or treated as a series of disconnected tasks.

For full-service support, Fatima works with a limited number of students so every application receives genuine time and attention, like having a strategist, mentor, and cheerleader in your corner.

Your application should sound like you

The strongest applications are not built from exaggerated achievements or stories created to impress. They come from understanding what matters to you and expressing it with clarity.

For postgraduate applicants, that may mean bringing greater focus to your ideas and long-term ambitions. For undergraduate applicants, it may mean exploring genuine interests and developing meaningful leadership.

Education can be the start of something bigger

We love working with students who want their education to lead somewhere meaningful. The right degree or scholarship can open doors to stronger research, better ideas, and work that benefits communities in Pakistan and beyond.

Your route, built with you

No two students bring the same background, ambitions, or questions, so their applications should not follow the same formula either. We help you understand your options, make informed decisions, and build an application that feels focused, honest, and unmistakably yours.
